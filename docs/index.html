CaseTrack USCIS

Privacy Policy

Overview

CaseTrack USCIS helps users check USCIS case updates. If you save cases in the app, receipt numbers and nicknames may be stored on your device so the app can show them again later.

This app is for informational purposes only. It does not provide legal advice, legal representation, or official USCIS decisions.

What Information May Be Used

The app may use USCIS receipt numbers entered by the user to request case-status updates through the app backend.

If the user saves a case, the receipt number, nickname, and latest status information may be stored locally on the device.
