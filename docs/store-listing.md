## App Title

CaseTrack USCIS

## Short Description

Track USCIS cases, save receipts, chat with AI support, and stay organized.

## Full Description

CaseTrack USCIS helps you track USCIS case progress in one clean mobile app.

Enter a USCIS receipt number to check the latest case status through a secure backend, review progress on a visual timeline, and save important cases for quick access later. The app is designed to make case tracking easier to understand and easier to revisit.

Key features:

- Track USCIS case status
- Save and manage multiple receipt numbers
- Refresh saved cases anytime
- Multilingual app support for English, Spanish, and Hindi
- Built-in AI support chat for general immigration guidance and app help
- Privacy Policy and Support information available in the app

CaseTrack USCIS is designed for convenience and clarity, especially for users who want one place to monitor updates across multiple cases.

Important notice:

- This app is for informational purposes only
- This app does not provide legal advice
- AI chat responses are generated by an AI assistant and are not a substitute for an immigration attorney
- This app is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by USCIS or the U.S. government
